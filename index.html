Bio
Cosme Chavez. I am an independent software developer with over 4 years of experience based in San Diego, California.
I migrated from Xalapa, Ver, Mexico in 2013. This transition allowed me to prove myself. A 3-year long transition that let me think about my passions and the things that matter in my life, my present and future life. I developed a passion for computers at an early age from looking at my step-father work with computers. My passion lead me to teach myself, and pratice my skills. My motivation came from my passion and curiosity that eventually lead into my career. I saw the world of computing and engineering differently; I did not see it as a job. Computing was more than that: it was my art, it was my will, a part of my soul.
I saw my ability to create software as a method of expression, an escape, but especially, a method to create something unique. I take pride my my work, I believe that my effort has to be meaningful, regardless of how small or big my operation is.
