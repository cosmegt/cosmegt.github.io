Bio
Cosme Chavez is an independent software developer with over 4 years of experience based in San Diego, California.
He migrated from Mexico in 2013 and developed a passion for computers at an early age. His passion lead him to teach himself, and pratice his skills. His motivation came from his passion and curiosity that eventually lead into a career. He saw the world of computing and engineering differently; he did not see it as a job. Computing was more than that: it was his art, it was his will.
He saw his ability to create software as a method of expression, an escape, but especially, a method to create something unique. He takes pride in his work, he believes his effort has to be meaningful, regardless of how small or big his operation is.
